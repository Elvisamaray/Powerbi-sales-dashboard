# Grocery Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## Executive Overview
This project delivers an executive level Power BI dashboard designed to evaluate grocery sales performance for BlinkIT. The objective was to convert raw transactional data into actionable insights that support strategic decision-making across product assortment, outlet performance, and sales optimization.

The dashboard prioritizes clarity, interactivity, and business relevance, enabling stakeholders to quickly identify revenue drivers and performance gaps.

---

## Business Objective
The primary objectives of this analysis were to:
- Provide a consolidated view of overall sales performance
- Evaluate how product attributes (fat content and item type) influence revenue
- Assess outlet performance across size, type, and establishment year
- Enable rapid, insight-driven decisions through an intuitive dashboard design

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard tracks and analyzes the following core business metrics:

- **Total Sales**
- **Average Sales per Item**
- **Number of Items Sold**
- **Average Customer Rating**

All KPIs are dynamically linked across visuals to support interactive and scenario-based analysis.

---

## Analytical Insights
Key findings from the analysis include:
- Regular fat products account for a significantly larger share of total revenue compared to low fat alternatives.
- Fruits, snacks, and household items are the strongest revenue generating product categories.
- Medium sized outlets consistently outperform small and large outlets in total sales.
- Outlets established earlier demonstrate stronger performance, suggesting the influence of brand maturity and customer trust.

## Dashboard Design Approach
- Single page executive layout for high level decision making
- Insight driven visuals rather than descriptive charts
- Consistent formatting and clean design principles
- Interactive slicers to support exploratory analysis
- Use of matrix visuals for comprehensive KPI comparison

---

## Strategic Recommendations
Based on the analysis, the following actions are recommended:
- Prioritize inventory and promotions for high-performing product categories such as fruits and snacks.
- Leverage regular-fat product sales trends for targeted pricing and merchandising strategies.
- Use medium-sized outlets as operational benchmarks for performance optimization.
- Expand future analysis to include geographic and regional performance drivers.
